Hamari Legacy

Dhok Malik Mehmand ki bunyad Malik Mehmand ne rakhi thi. Yeh ilaka sadiyon se apni pur-waqar riwayaat aur bahadur logon ki wajah se jana jata hai. Yahan ki matti ne har shobay mein numayan kaam karne wale log paida kiye hain.

Hamari kahani sirf purani imaraton ya zameenon tak mehdood nahi, balki yeh mehmaan nawazi, bhaicharay aur mehnat ki kahani hai. Is gaon mein puranay dor se hi taleem aur deen ko aik khaas muqam hasil raha hai, jis ki zinda misal Jamia Masjid Gulzar-e-Madina hai.

Hum chahte hain ke hamara gaon technology ke medan mein bhi apni aik alag pehchan banaye.
